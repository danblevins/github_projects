Sign up for all 3 Services

Resume & Cover Letter

  • 1. We first hold a 30 minute phone call to learn about YOU and the role you want
  • 2. We edit and build upon your resume or cover letter
  • 3. We focus on keywords, strengthen your experiences and skills, and optimize space
  • 4. We share research and articles that empower you to craft competitive resumes and cover letters in the future
  • 5. In 3 weeks, you have a flashy new resume or cover letter and resources to keep it up-to-date
Sign Up Now

LinkedIn Profile

  • 1. We first hold a 30 minute phone call to learn about YOU and the role you want
  • 2. We give you ideas on how to create your LinkedIn by designing a mock profile for you
  • 3. We provide you with guidance on how to communicate, interact, and engage with professionals on LinkedIn
  • 4. We share research and articles that help you to keep your LinkedIn updated
  • 5. In 3 weeks, you have a LinkedIn profile and ways to connect and network with other professionals
Sign Up Now

Interview Prep

  • 1. We're on standby to prepare you for your phone, virtual, or in-person interview. The sooner you let us know about your upcoming interview, the better
  • 2. We help you create a personalized prep packet that goes over typical questions
  • 3. We give you ideas on how to use your resume, LinkedIn, or cover letter to your advantage
  • 4. We assist you in holding 30 minute mock interviews, if desired
  • 5. We help you plan questions to ask the interviewer, including how and when to follow up
